Lasagna

Decription

This is a simple and easy delicious recipe to make the lovely lasagna pasta. A combination of meat, tomato sauce and sheet pasta to make this yummy meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Beaten Egg
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente
  2. Saute ground beef, sausage, onion and garlic
  3. Stir in pasta sauce, tomato paste, italian seasoning, salt and pepper. Simmer until thickened
  4. In a bowl, combine mozzarella, parmesan, ricotta, parsley, egg and salt.
  5. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in 9x13 pan. Top with 3 lasagna noodles and then ricotta cheese and meat sauce. Repeat
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes
  7. Remove foil and sprinkle remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese. Bake for 15 more minutes
  8. ENJOY!!!
