Carbonara Pasta

Decription

This is a simple and easy delicious recipe to make the lovely carbonar pasta. A combination of butter, bacon and pasta to make this yummy meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Bacon
  • Garlic
  • Whole Eggs
  • Parmesan
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the pasta water
  2. Saute the bacon and garlic
  3. Beat the eggs and half of the cheese
  4. Cook pasta
  5. Toss the pasta with bacon
  6. Add the beaten egg mixture
  7. ENJOY!!!
