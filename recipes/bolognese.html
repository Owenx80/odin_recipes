Bolognese Pasta

Decription

This is a simple and easy delicious recipe to make the lovely bolognese pasta. A combination of meat, tomato sauce and pasta to make this yummy meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Heavey Cream
  • Ground Beef
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Saute the carrots, celery and onion
  2. Stir in the ground beef
  3. Pour in your jar of marinar sauce
  4. Simmer! About 30 minutes will let it thicken and cook down
  5. Boil pasta during the simmering phase
  6. When sauce has reached desired thickness, add heavy cream
  7. Combine the bolognese sauce with al dente pasta
  8. ENJOY!!!
